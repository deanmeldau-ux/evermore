Good to know

Questions, answered

Is it really free?

Yes — creating your wedding website, sharing it, collecting RSVPs and your photo wall are free to begin. You'll only ever pay if you choose a premium add-on later.

How do guests RSVP?

You share one link. Guests open your invitation in any browser and reply right there — with their headcount, song request and dietary needs. It all lands in your dashboard.

What's the QR code for?

It's for the wedding day. Pop it on your tables and guests scan to add the photos they take straight to your private gallery — no app to download.

Do my guests need an account?

Never. Viewing the invite, RSVPing and sharing photos all work with a simple link or scan.
